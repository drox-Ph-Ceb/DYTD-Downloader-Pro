🎸 Chord Analyzer Pro

Real-time guitar chord recognition with live diagrams, progression tracking, and an expandable Pro Chord Viewer.

  1. Search or paste a YouTube link, then download as Audio (MP3).
  2. Open Audio Library and play your track.
  3. Watch the live chord name, key, and progression in the chord panel below the sidebar controls.
  4. Toggle Deep for more accurate analysis, or Easy for simpler diatonic chords.
  5. Set Capo (0–11) to match your guitar fretboard.
  6. Click the chord display to open the enlarged Pro Chord Viewer popup.
💡 Pro Chord Viewer includes: large fretboard diagram, per-string fret & note breakdown (E → e), chord tones, key scale with Roman numerals, diatonic chord family, live sync during playback, and highlighted scale notes for the current chord.
